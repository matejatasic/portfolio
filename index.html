About Me

Hello there! My name is Mateja Tasic and I am a Fullstack developer with over 2 year of professional experience. I am currently working full-time for a digital sports media group as a Fullstack developer with some part-time freelance work designing and developing websites. Since the time I discovered the power of programming and how it can help people by solving many of their problems a couple of years ago, I have constantly been improving my skills striving to build better, more maintainable, faster, scalable and, above all, a software that solves the problems of specific people and businesses. To achieve this I understood that I needed to learn from a various fields of knowledge, which would help me understand and contribute to as much stages of software development as I possibly can. I am far from the end of that journey, but I am looking forward to tackling the problems that will bring me more closer to the end and help more people and businesses along the way.

Freelance projects

Kick boxing club website

A website I made for a local kick boxing club. Users can book their first training, book individual trainings, view when the training sessions are held, view news related to the club and get email notifications regarding their membership fees.

Personal projects

Food delivery app

An app that allows you to order food from a variety of restaurants easily. Payout is done using Stripe. The app also has the driver part, where drivers can take orders, can view the route to the destination, mark the orders as completed.

Cab management system

This project is in progress. The app allows customers to seamlessly rent cabs. It will also have an admin dashboard where where managers from different departments will be able to see, create, update and delete information related to their department.

Bank management system

This is a simple console app that allows basic customer functionalities related to banking. The app also has the admin part with functionalities related to the employees, account and transactions. The app is built in such a way that it can easily be converted to a web, mobile or even a desktop app.

Chat

This is a simple chat application that allows users to receive messages in real time, either in the public chat, where everyone can see the messages, or in the private chat between to users.

Budget app

An app that enables you to enter your budget, enter, delete and edit your expenses and calculate the balance. All data is stored in local storage so you can always see your budget.

Devhire app

An app that enables you add, edit and delete a developer and hire developers individually or as a team. All data is stored in local storage so you can always see your developers and hires.

Flashcards app

An app that enables you to enter a question and an answer, which are stored in local storage, show them in a flash card and edit or delete a flashcard.

BMI Calculator

An app that enables you to choose which units to use, enter your height and weight and get the results.
